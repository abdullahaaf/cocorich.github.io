Coconut Charcoal Briquette is a compact charcoal beam which is made from coconut shell char-coal. Our charcoal briquettes are made from crushed coconut shell charcoal and molded using nat-ural binders. As a fuel, this charcoal is hotter and lasts longer than ordinary charcoal. It is suitable for household use both indoors and outdoors. Also, it can be used in the food industry, for barbe-cue, stove fuel, metallurgy, etc.



Our shisha briquettes are made with 100% natural ingredients. The coconut shell ensures easy igni-tion, high and even heat and longer burning time.



CocoRich Indonesia guarantees environmental safety from raw coconut shell collection to packag-ing and shipping. We harvest our coconut shells sustainably, without harming palm trees, animal habitats, or the en-vironment. We use tapioca flour to bind charcoal briquettes, which is a 100% food grade product extracted from the roots of the Manihot Esculenta (cassava) plant.
